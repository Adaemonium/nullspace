<ul id="rules">
	<li>
		<h1>1. Accounts</h1>
		<p>a. Each player is only permitted to control a single account.
		</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<h1>2. Pushing</h1>
		<p>b. It is not permitted for any account to obtain profit out of a lower ranked account in any matter regarding resources or manipulation of fleets.
This includes but is not limited to:

<br />- Resources sent from a lower ranked account to a higher ranked one with nothing tangible in return.
<br />- A player crashing his fleet into a higher ranked one for the higher ranked one to retain the Debris Field.
<br />- Loans that are not returned within 48 hours.
<br />- Trades in which the higher ranked player does not exchange the resources within 48 hours.
<br />- A higher ranked player participating in any form of extortion.
<br />- Trades that fall outside the following ratio:
<br />3:2:1 Where each unit of deuterium is worth 2 units of crystal or 3 units of metal.</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<h1>3. Bashing</h1>
		<p>c. It is not permitted to attack any given planet or moon owned by a player over 6 times in a single 24 hour period.
<br />The bashing rule only applies for active users and excludes misile attacks and espionage missions.</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<h1>4. Exploiting Bugs</h1>
		<p>d. Using a bug for anyones profit intentionally or not reporting a bug intentionally is strictly forbidden.</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<h1>5. Content</h1>
		<p>e. When chatting and communicating:
<br />- Be respectful - no harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
<br />- No Spamming – Avoid flooding chat with repeated messages, excessive emojis, or irrelevant content.
<br />- No Illegal Content – Do not share pirated material, threats, or anything illegal.
<br />- Use Appropriate Language – Avoid offensive or NSFW language
<br />- Respect Decisions – Moderators and admins have the final say.
<br />- Report Problems Calmly – If issues arise, report them and maintain patience.</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<h1>6. Violations</h1>
		<p>In the event that the rules above are violated once or more times, an administrator will take the following action:
		<br /> <br />a. Multi-accounting: Complete removal of all accounts.
		<br /> <br />b. Pushing: Suspension of account for 24 hours. 24 hours are appended to subsequent violations up to 96 hours (3 times), followed by complete removal of account.
		<br /> <br />c. Suspension of account for 24 hours with 24 hours appended for subsequent violations up to 96 hours (3 times), followed by complete removal of account.
		<br /> <br />d. Depending on severity: suspension of 96 hours, or complete removal of account.
		<br /> <br />e. Suspension of account for 24 hours with 24 hours appended for subsequent violations. Content or behaviour deemed extremely unacceptable or illegal will result in a permanent ban. </p>
	</li>
</ul>
